Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Yuanhang Cross-Border ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you interact with our website, services, or advertisements on platforms such as Facebook (Meta).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, Telegram/WhatsApp handle, and any other information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms, Facebook Lead Ads, or direct communications.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, referring URLs, and pages visited on our website through cookies and similar tracking technologies (including the Meta Pixel).
  • Advertising Data: Information about your interactions with our ads on Facebook and other platforms, which helps us measure ad performance and improve targeting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the services you request (e.g., advertising consultation, quotes, and campaign management).
  • To communicate with you about your inquiries, updates, and promotional offers.
  • To improve our website, advertising, and marketing strategies through analytics and performance tracking.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate our business (e.g., CRM systems, email marketing platforms, analytics providers like Google Analytics). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they perform for us.
  • Advertising Platforms: We share limited data (such as email hashes or phone numbers) with Facebook (Meta) and Google for the purpose of creating custom audiences and measuring ad performance, in compliance with their respective terms.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, us, or others.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (including the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics) to:

  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Track conversions and measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.
  • Retarget users who have interacted with our website or ads.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. To opt out of personalized advertising on Facebook, you can adjust your Facebook Ad Preferences.

5. Your Privacy Rights (Under CCPA and Other U.S. Laws)

If you are a resident of California or other U.S. states with similar privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You can request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, as well as the purposes for which we use that information.
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete the personal information we hold about you, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal compliance).
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information for targeted advertising. We do not sell your data, but you can opt out of behavioral advertising through your browser settings or ad platform preferences.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 8 below.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
